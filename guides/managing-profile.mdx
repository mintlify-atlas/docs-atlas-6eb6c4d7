---
title: 'Managing your profile'
description: 'How to view and update your TravelConnect profile, see your enrolled trips, and request a Creator promotion.'
---

Your profile page shows your account information and lets you update your display name and bio.

## Viewing your profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the profile page">
    Go to `/profile`. The page fetches your account data from:

    ```http
    GET /users/users/me
    Authorization: Bearer <your_jwt_token>
    ```

    The response includes your `email`, `full_name`, `bio`, `rol`, and `id`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review your information">
    Your profile displays the following fields:

    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | **Email** | Your registered email address (read-only) |
    | **Full name** (`full_name`) | Your display name |
    | **Bio** | A short personal description |
    | **Current role** (`rol`) | `Viatger`, `Creador`, or `Admin` |
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Updating your display name and bio

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Editar Perfil">
    On your profile page, click the **Editar Perfil** button to switch to the edit form.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update your details">
    Modify your **Nombre Completo** (`full_name`) and/or **Biografía** (`bio`) fields.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Guardar Cambios**. The frontend sends a `PUT` request with your new name and bio URL-encoded as path parameters:

    ```http
    PUT /users/users/me/{nom}/{bio}
    Authorization: Bearer <your_jwt_token>
    ```

    For example, updating to the name "Joan" and bio "Hiking enthusiast" would call:

    ```http
    PUT /users/users/me/Joan/Hiking%20enthusiast
    Authorization: Bearer <your_jwt_token>
    ```

    On success, your profile page updates immediately to reflect the new values.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The `full_name` field is required when saving changes. The `bio` field is optional — you can leave it empty.
</Note>

## Viewing your enrolled trips

Your profile page reflects your current account state. To see the trips you have joined, return to the dashboard at `/dashboard`. Trips you are enrolled in will show an **Inscrito** status on their detail pages.

## Requesting a Creator promotion

If your current role is `Viatger` and you want to create trips, you can submit a promotion request to an Admin.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm your current role">
    Check the **Rol Actual** field on your profile page at `/profile`. It must show `Viatger` to be eligible for a promotion request.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit a promotion request">
    Send a `POST` request to the promotion endpoint with a message explaining your intent:

    ```http
    POST /viatge/promote-request
    Authorization: Bearer <your_jwt_token>
    Content-Type: application/json

    {
      "missatge": "I want to organise cultural tours across Europe."
    }
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for approval">
    An Admin will review your request. Once approved, your role is updated to `Creador`. Log out and log back in to receive an updated token reflecting your new role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  After your role is upgraded to `Creador`, the **+ Crear Nuevo Viaje** button will appear on the dashboard and you can begin publishing trips. See [Creating trips](/guides/creating-trips) for the full workflow.
</Tip>
