The trips dashboard is the central hub for discovering travel experiences on TravelConnect. You must be logged in to access it.

## Navigating to the dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your account">
    Complete the login flow at `/login`. On success you are redirected automatically to `/dashboard`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the trips list">
    The dashboard loads the **Explorar Viajes** page, which fetches all available trips from the API:

    ```http
    GET /viatge/trips
    ```

    Trips are returned as a list and rendered as individual trip cards in a responsive grid.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  `/dashboard` is a protected route. If you navigate to it without a valid session, you will be redirected to the login page.
</Note>

## Browsing the trip cards

Each card in the grid represents one trip and displays a summary of its key details. The grid adapts across screen sizes â€” one column on mobile, two on medium screens, and three on large screens.

If no trips are available, the page displays: *"No hay viajes disponibles en este momento."*

## Group chat

Once you are enrolled in a trip, a **Chat del Grupo** tab appears on the detail page. Admins can always see the chat tab regardless of enrollment. Trip chat messages are fetched from `/missatges/trips/:id/chat`.

## Creating a new trip

If your account has the `Creador` or `Admin` role, a **+ Crear Nuevo Viaje** button appears in the top-right corner of the trips list. Click it to navigate to `/trips/create`.

<Tip>
  Only `Creador` and `Admin` accounts can create trips. If you are a `Viatger` and want to create trips, request a promotion from your profile page. See [Managing your profile](/guides/managing-profile).
</Tip>
