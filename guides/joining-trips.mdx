---
title: 'Joining trips'
description: 'How to enroll in a trip, leave a trip, and request a Creator promotion on TravelConnect.'
---

As a `Viatger`, you can join any trip that has available capacity and leave it at any time.

## Enrolling in a trip

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the trip detail page">
    From the dashboard at `/dashboard`, click on the trip you want to join to open its detail page at `/trips/:id`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check the available spots">
    In the sidebar, confirm that the enrolled count is less than the maximum capacity. For example:

    ```
    Capacity:  12 people
    Enrolled:  7
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Inscribirse ahora">
    Click the **Inscribirse ahora** button. This sends a `POST` request to the API:

    ```http
    POST /viatge/trips/{id}/enroll
    Authorization: Bearer <your_jwt_token>
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm enrollment">
    After a successful enrollment, the **Inscribirse ahora** button is replaced by an **Inscrito** confirmation badge. The participant count in the sidebar updates to reflect the new total.

    The **Chat del Grupo** tab also becomes visible on the detail page, giving you access to the group conversation for that trip.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The **Inscribirse ahora** button is only visible to users with the `Viatger` role. `Creador` and `Admin` users manage trips rather than enrolling in them.
</Note>

## Leaving a trip

To withdraw from a trip you have already joined:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the trip detail page">
    Go to `/trips/:id` for the trip you want to leave.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Leave the trip">
    Use the leave option. The frontend sends:

    ```http
    POST /viatge/trips/{id}/leave
    Authorization: Bearer <your_jwt_token>
    ```

    Your enrollment is removed and the participant count decreases by one.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When a trip is full

<Warning>
  If a trip has reached its `maxim_participants` limit, the enrollment request will fail with a `400` error. The server returns an error message indicating the trip is full. Check other trips on the dashboard for available spots.
</Warning>

## Requesting a Creator promotion

If you want to create and manage your own trips, you can submit a promotion request to be upgraded from `Viatger` to `Creador`.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your profile">
    Navigate to `/profile` and confirm your current role is `Viatger`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit a promotion request">
    Use the promotion request feature. The frontend sends a `POST` request with a message explaining why you want the Creator role:

    ```http
    POST /viatge/promote-request
    Authorization: Bearer <your_jwt_token>
    Content-Type: application/json

    {
      "missatge": "I want to organise group hiking trips in the Alps."
    }
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for admin approval">
    An Admin will review your request. Once approved, your role is updated to `Creador` and you can begin creating trips. You will need to log out and log back in for the new role to be reflected in your session token.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Write a clear and specific message in your promotion request describing the types of trips you plan to create. This helps admins process requests faster.
</Tip>
